Antoni Patek and His Watches

Patek Phillipe

Antoni Patek is one of the Co founders of the most luxurious watch company "Patek Phillipe". Patek Phillipe creates some of the most lavish watches on the planet, handmade with precision and taking anywhere from 1 to 8 years of development and production. Prices Ranging lowest as 50,000 up to 25 million. Almost no other watch brands come to this level of precision and luxury.
